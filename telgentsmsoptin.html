SMS Opt-in Workflow

Business: Telgent
Website: https://www.telgent.ai
Use Case: Conversational/Customer Support & Business Updates
1 Customer Visits Website or Contacts Business

Entry Points:

  • Website: https://www.telgent.ai/contact
  • Phone consultation
  • Service inquiry form
⬇️
2 Digital Opt-in Form Presented

SMS Consent Form (Located at: https://www.telgent.ai/sms-consent)

Compliance Notes:
  • Checkboxes are unchecked by default
  • SMS consent is separate from email consent
  • Form is branded with Telgent name
  • Clear opt-out instructions provided
⬇️
3 Customer Submits Form with Consent

Customer checks appropriate boxes and submits form with phone number and consent preferences.

⬇️
4 Welcome/Confirmation Message Sent
Example Welcome Message:
"Welcome to Telgent SMS updates! You're now subscribed to receive [transactional/marketing] messages. Reply STOP to unsubscribe, HELP for assistance. Msg&data rates may apply. Frequency varies."
⬇️
5 Ongoing Message Types

Transactional Messages:

  • Service appointment reminders
  • Project status updates
  • Support ticket notifications
  • Account alerts

Marketing Messages (if opted in):

  • New service announcements
  • Special offers
  • Company news
⬇️
6 Opt-out Process
Customer texts: "STOP"
Auto-response: "You have been unsubscribed from Telgent SMS messages. You will no longer receive texts from this number. Reply START to resubscribe."

Additional Compliance Information:

  • Message Frequency: Transactional as needed, Marketing 2-4 times per month maximum
  • Operating Hours: Messages sent between 8 AM - 8 PM local time
  • Privacy Policy: Includes language stating "We will not share or sell your mobile information with third parties for promotional or marketing purposes"
  • Help Keyword: Reply HELP for: "Telgent SMS Help: Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Contact support@telgent.ai or call [phone number] for assistance."
